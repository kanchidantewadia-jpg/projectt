Reuniting Lost Loved Ones

ReUn is a community-driven platform that helps find missing persons without relying on phones or GPS. Together, we can bring families back together.

How ReUn Works

Our innovative approach combines community efforts with technology to locate missing persons

Report Missing

Submit detailed information about the missing person including photos, last known location, and physical description.

Alert Community

Our system notifies volunteers and community members in the area where the person was last seen.

Organize Search

Coordinate search efforts with local authorities and volunteers using our mapping tools.

Community Support

Leverage the power of community with tips, sightings, and volunteer search parties.
